## About The Project
Habitat Finance solves the fragmentation of modern wealth by unifying traditional bank deposits, private equity, and digital assets into a single, mathematically rigorous dashboard. 

## How It Solves the Problem
Instead of relying on basic account aggregation and self-reported risk questionnaires, our solution standardizes disparate asset classes and applies quantitative finance models to assess true financial health. Our backend calculates risk-adjusted metrics like the Sharpe Ratio and plots the user's unified portfolio against an Efficient Frontier graph. 

Crucially, our platform features a "Macro Stress-Tester." Users can simulate market shocks (e.g., interest rate hikes), and our AI-powered recommendation engine instantly suggests exact, personalized portfolio rebalancing actions to optimize returns and ensure behavioral resilience. Designed with a B2B2C dual-view toggle, it empowers both retail clients with intuitive visualizations and wealth advisors with deep, actionable analytics.
